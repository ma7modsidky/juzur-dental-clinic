Our Services

Veneers

veneer installation service to beautify worn and broken teeth and get rid of gaps and pigmentation in a quick time, with unparalleled quality, and in a 100% natural shape and color.

Dental crown installation

Installing dental crowns is a safe solution to preserve molars after nerve filling operations, to protect them from fractures or corrosion. The crowns are manufactured in our own laboratories in only 40 minutes.

Dental implants

Replacing missing teeth resulting from fractures and decay to maintain gum health through dental implants using advanced techniques and methods. The formula is manufactured in our laboratories at breakneck speed.

Teeth whitening

By removing pigmentation, returning the teeth to their natural color, and unifying the color for a new, attractive smile.

Root canal therapy

a dental procedure to remove inflamed or infected pulp on the inside of the tooth which is then carefully cleaned and disinfected.

Tooth extraction

a dental procedure during which your tooth is completely removed from its socket. Sometimes, people refer to this as “pulling" a tooth.
